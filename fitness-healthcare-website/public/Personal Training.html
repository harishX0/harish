Weekly Workout and Diet Plan

Beginner Level

Day Workout Diet
Monday Bodyweight Circuit (Squats, Push-ups, Plank) Oatmeal with banana, nuts, and honey
Tuesday Cardio (Brisk Walking or Cycling) Greek yogurt with berries and honey
Wednesday Rest or Light Yoga Grilled chicken with steamed vegetables
Thursday Bodyweight Workout (Squats, Lunges, Dips) Eggs with whole-grain toast and avocado
Friday Cardio (Jogging or Cycling) Quinoa salad with mixed greens and chickpeas
Saturday Core Workout (Planks, Russian Twists, Leg Raises) Fruit smoothie with protein powder
Sunday Rest or Light Stretching Light meals or snacks as needed

Intermediate Level

Day Workout Diet
Monday Upper Body Strength (Bench Press, Rows, Push-ups) Protein shake with spinach and oats
Tuesday Lower Body Strength (Squats, Lunges, Deadlifts) Grilled salmon with quinoa and spinach
Wednesday HIIT Cardio (Sprints, Jump Rope, Burpees) Chicken stir-fry with brown rice
Thursday Full Body Circuit Training Turkey sandwich on whole-grain bread
Friday Cardio (Swimming or Running) Mixed fruit salad with nuts
Saturday Core & Flexibility (Pilates, Yoga) Omelet with spinach and mushrooms
Sunday Rest or Light Activity Snack as needed

Advanced Level

Day Workout Diet
Monday Chest & Triceps (Bench Press, Dips, Cable Flys) Eggs, avocado, and whole-grain toast
Tuesday Back & Biceps (Pull-ups, Barbell Rows, Curls) Protein shake with almond milk and berries
Wednesday HIIT Cardio (Jump Rope, Sprints, Battle Ropes) Quinoa bowl with chicken and veggies
Thursday Legs & Shoulders (Squats, Deadlifts, Military Press) Grilled chicken with quinoa and steamed broccoli
Friday Full Body Powerlifting (Deadlifts, Squats, Bench Press) Salmon with roasted sweet potatoes and asparagus
Saturday Core & Conditioning (Planks, Russian Twists, Leg Raises) Cottage cheese with pineapple and almonds
Sunday Active Recovery (Light Jogging or Yoga) Light meals or snacks as needed
